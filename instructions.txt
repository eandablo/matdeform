This 3D tic tac toe consist of three floors of the classic game.
Your oponent will be the computer/machine (M).
You will be asked to provide three numbers between 1 and 3.
First number selects the floor: 1 (Bottom), 2 (mid), 3 (Top).
Second and third correspond to the coordenates of rows and columns
within floor, respectively.
Your (Y) selection will be marked with a Y.
Computer/machine moves first, marking the selection as M
To win the game you need either of the following
- Win two floors as in a conventional tic tac toe or,
- Connect the three floors with a vertical line
